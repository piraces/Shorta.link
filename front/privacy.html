shorta.link Privacy Policy

We do not collect any personally identifying information from users of shorta.link. We store only the information necessary to provide our URL shortening service (such as original long URLs and source IP for rate limiting purposes).

URLs shortened by shorta.link are not private and should not be treated as such. Third parties could easily guess the short URL that you are using, so you should not use shorta.link to link to sensitive or secure data.
